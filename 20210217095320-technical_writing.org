I've been listening to the U Chicago Writing Lab's class on "the craft of writing effectively" and it's got me thinking about the theory of power in writing.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vtIzMaLkCaM

Their program for building influential, /valuable/ writing is:
- Find who has power in your community
- Write what they want to hear, WHILE ALSO challenging them in a /valuable/ way.
